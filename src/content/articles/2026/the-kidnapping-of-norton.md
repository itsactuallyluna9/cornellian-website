---
title: The Kidnapping of Norton
author: Pine Tree
pubDate: Apr 1, 2026
---

On March 16th, 2026, witnesses reported an incident in our Cole Library. At midnight, Norton the Dinosaur was being mysteriously taken away by about five strong and adorably dressed… squirrels. The Cornellian was immediately on the scene, looking for traces of where Norton was kidnapped. The only clue that was found was the left-behind acorns that spelled “Beware.” One of the investigators on the scene actually said it spelled“Bewarb,” but he was quickly fired for bringing in too many Gravity Falls references in one day. The next night, the security camera captured one of the most shocking heists in Cornell College history. Norton the Dinosaur was brought back by those same thieves—except, he is now more polished than ever! His green skin was unexpectedly clean, except for maybe a few crumbs of food here and there. The thieves left behind another note made from acorns, “Till’ next year.”
