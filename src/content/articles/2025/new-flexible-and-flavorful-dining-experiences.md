---
title: "New Flexible and Flavorful Dining Experiences"
author: Murray Page, Rhye Nelson, Clara Biela, James Belding, and Luna
pubDate: Nov 19, 2025
---
Starting this spring semester, Bon Appétit will have changes to their schedule. Beginning on January 11, 2026, there will no longer be Saturday dinner or breakfast at the Hilltop Café. However, Saturday will receive an extension to its lunch hours. From 11 a.m. to 2 p.m., the cafeteria will be open for Saturday brunch to encourage “sleeping in or catching up with friends later in the day” (Bon Appétit Cornell College). Instead of Saturday dinner, Zamora’s Market will be open from 4 p.m. to 6 p.m. to accommodate for the lost meal. Bon Appétit claims that these later hours at Zamora’s will have an “expanded menu” (Bon Appétit Cornell College).

While all other times will remain the same, the options for meal plans will be changing. Instead of the default meal plan being 20 swipes a week with $0 flex dollars, the new default will be 18 meal swipes and $75 flex dollars to use at Zamora’s Market. Doing the math, that equates to approximately $5.36 per week to accommodate for the loss of two meals. The 14 meal swipes plan will remain the same, while a new option for 9 meal swipes will now be available to everyone. This new plan will allow for 9 weekly meal swipes and $400 flex dollars. Students can select the meal plans that “best fits their routine” (Bon Appétit Cornell College) for Spring 2026, beginning on November 17, 2025.

Two vending machines will be available in the SAW lobby and second floor of Thomas Commons. Both vending machines will be filled with to-go sandwhiches, salads, meal replacements, and snacks (created by Bon Appétit) that can be paid for with flex dollars or debit/credit cards. “Get ready for a more flexible and flavorful dining experience this spring!” (Bon Appétit Cornell College)
